The Process

From social signals to the right card in four steps

Our pipeline transforms unstructured behavioral data into a structured, explainable financial profile that banks can act on.

STEP 01
🔌
Connect Your Socials
Grant read-only access to your public profiles. We never post, never store credentials, and never read private messages.
STEP 02
🧠
Signal Extraction
Our NLP models parse engagement patterns, sentiment trajectories, posting cadence, and network quality across platforms.
STEP 03
⚖️
Bias-Checked Scoring
Each feature vector passes through our fairness filter — checking for demographic proxy signals before contributing to your score.
STEP 04
💳
Matched Recommendations
Your trust profile is matched against real card products using a ranking model trained on 500K approval outcomes.

Privacy & Fairness

Ethical by design, not by afterthought

Every technical decision in TrustCard's pipeline was made with one question in mind: would this be fair if applied to someone who looks nothing like the training data?

✓ Audited against 14 demographic bias dimensions
✓ Data deleted within 30 days of analysis
✓ Full explainability — know exactly why you got your score
🔒
Read-Only Access
We request the minimum OAuth scopes. No posting, no DM access, no credential storage.
🧹
Data Minimization
Raw posts are processed in-memory and immediately discarded. Only vectorized signals are retained.
⚖️
Fairness Filters
Protected attributes (gender, location, language) are explicitly removed before scoring.
📋
Full Explainability
Every score comes with a human-readable breakdown. No black boxes — you can dispute any signal.
